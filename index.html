About the Artist

Jamie Cain is a fine artist and painter whose work captures the atmospheric beauty of Florida's landscapes. A Florida native now based in Jupiter, she works primarily in acrylic, oil, and gouache.

Her subjects range from abstracted landscapes and aerial cloud studies to figurative work, all unified by a deep appreciation for natural light and the quiet moments that often go unnoticed.

After years of studying color theory, drawing, and painting through workshops across the Southeast, Jamie now paints full time, bringing her philosophy of "art as lifestyle" to every canvas.
