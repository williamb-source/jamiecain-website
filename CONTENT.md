## Artist Bio (from web search results)

Jamie Cain is a fine artist and painter based in Jupiter, Florida. A Florida native who recently moved full time to Jupiter, FL, she paints with acrylic, oil, and gouache. Her beachtown roots have inspired her subject matter and style.

She paints abstracted landscapes, aerial cloud work and some figurative work. The skies and colors of Florida are a constant source of inspiration for her work.

### Background

Living in Georgia for over 25 years, she continued to learn new techniques by attending workshops and taking classes in color theory, drawing and painting. After raising her three sons, she began to paint full time.

### Philosophy

Although painter Jamie Cain has been pursuing art for most of her life, it wasn't until the last five years that she decided to become a full-time artist.
